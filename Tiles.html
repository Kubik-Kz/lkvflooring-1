TILES

For many reasons, there are a lot of tile flooring options available these days. Tile comes in many shapes, sizes, and colors. Tile also comes in different material such as porcelain or ceramic. At LKV flooring, we can supply and install your desired tiles.

Tile flooring options can be overwhelming, the key is to decide what you want and need and what is going to be most essential to you when the job is complete. Tile could be heated with a heating system that goes down under the floor before the tile is installed; this is a great tile flooring option especially in the winter.

Tile floors can be simple to do, or they can be a real struggle. Your taste and style are going to determine the tile installation process. Also, the slab has to be level, or proper steps will have to be made to make it level. You have a choice between using a concrete based board or hardy backer table, or you can go old school and have your floors done with a concrete mud base which may not be easier but is usually for sure the most efficient way to make sure your tile flooring lasts the longest.
