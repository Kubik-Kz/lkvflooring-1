About us

Welcome to LKV Flooring, home of the best flooring services in the city of Brisbane, Gold Coast and surrounded areas in Queensland. We provide top-notch Timber Flooring, Tiles, Vinyl Planks, Floor Sanding and Polishing, sourced from the best of quality materials available. We maintain our distinctive quality in all projects, with flawless floor finishes to add aesthetics to all kinds of interior designs and buildings.

LKV Flooring provides world-class services to both residential and commercial buildings, in a timely fashion and excellent feedback. Highly experienced and dedicated to giving your home or office a brand-new outlook. Our team of sub-contractors consists of well-trained and highly competent individuals, always ready to get the job done fast and effectively. We offer the best flooring options, utilizing the latest technology and expertise. We take pride in our high-class customer support, happy to listen to all the details and specifications you require for your new flooring. You can give us a call anytime for a free quote.
